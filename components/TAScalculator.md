This is what you earn every month as long as they stay active.

**Model 1 Expert (The 1 Expert)**
* Model 1 pays a flat **₦1,000 per month** regardless of how many jobs they do.
* **Total:** **₦1,000**

**Model 2 Experts (The 3 Experts)**

* For 3 experts: 3 × ₦1,500.
* **Total:** **₦4,500**

> **Total Monthly Recurring:** ₦1,000 + ₦4,500 = **₦5,500 / month**
